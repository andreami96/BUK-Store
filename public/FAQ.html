Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register?

Fill out your name and email address on the account registration form to get started. We will send you an email to confirm your account. Then you will be able to login and reserve books.

How do I find a book?

Go to the catalogue page and you will be prompted with all the books available in our bookstore. You can also add a filter on the theme or genre of the book you are looking for.

What is a book Genre?

A book Genre is a category of literary composition. Genres may be determined by literary technique, tone, content, or even (as in the case of fiction) length. The distinctions between genres and categories are flexible and loosely defined, often with subgroups.
Examples of main literary genres are: novel, didactic, poetry and theatre . If you are still curious about books genres check out the Wikipedia definition.

What is a book Theme?

The most common contemporary understanding of theme is an idea or point that is central to a story, which can often be summed in a single word (for example, love, death, betrayal). Typical examples of themes of this type are conflict between the individual and society; coming of age; humans in conflict with technology; nostalgia; and the dangers of unchecked ambition.
If you are still curious about narrative genres check out the Wikipedia definition.

What is a book Event?

A book Event is an event where you have the chance to meet the author of a book and possibly buy a copy of the book that will be presented during such event. Further information about a specific event can be found inside the apposite page of the event.
Check out our homepage to see all the upcoming events.
